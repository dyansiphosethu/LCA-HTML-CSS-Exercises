The Rise of Artificial Intelligence in Everyday Life

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is changing how people live, work, and interact with technology. AI is used in virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa, recommendation systems on Netflix, and even healthcare systems for disease detection.

AI improves efficiency and convenience, but it also raises concerns about privacy, ethics, and job automation.

Why Blockchain Technology Matters

Blockchain is best known for powering cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, but it has applications far beyond digital money.

It provides secure, transparent, and decentralized record keeping, making it useful in finance, healthcare, and supply chain management.
